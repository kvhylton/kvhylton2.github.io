At Xan’s Vineyard and Winery, we believe that a bottle of wine is the start of an adventure. It's a vessel to discover a region. To understand a culture. And to learn the history of a place. Every bottle in our store has been vetted and tasted, and everyone on our staff knows its story. Our shop and tasting events are designed for wine lovers of all levels to broaden their knowledge and increase their appreciation of wine. Through our wine services, we want to make your life-long journey of collecting wines a safe and hassle-free experience.

Wine. It's what we do. It's what we love. Our passion for it fuels our endless quest to learn more about it and share everything we've experienced on the way.
Wine makes us feel great and lends itself to lots of fun. Fun at a meal. Fun at a bar. Fun at a restaurant. Fun at a party. Fun at the beach, on a boat, in the park, etc.
When you become a customer with us, you're becoming a part of our journey, and we're joining you on yours. Our shop and wide array of wine services are meant to be with you every step of the way.

Riesling

Riesling is a white grape variety which originated in the Rhine region. Riesling is an aromatic grape variety displaying flowery, almost perfumed, aromas as well as high acidity. It is used to make dry, semi-sweet, sweet, and sparkling white wines. Riesling wines are usually varietally pure and are seldom oaked.
